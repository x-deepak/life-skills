# Focus Management

### Question 1
What is Deep Work?

- Deep work is focusing on something without distraction.

### Question 2
According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?

- Schedule Distractions - Plan when you go online (use social media apps, etc.)
- Deep work ritual—try to do some amount of deep work daily at the same times.
- Evening shutdown—get proper sleep at the day's end.


### Question 3
How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?

- By avoiding distractions as much as possible, get your sleep schedule fixed and try to do focused work at regular sessions.

### Question 4
What are the dangers of social media, in brief?

- Losing the ability to sustain concentration and psychological effects such as feeling more lonely, isolated, or depressed.

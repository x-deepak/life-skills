# Learning process


### Q1. What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line.

- Explaining something in simple terms as if you are teaching it to someone else.  


### Q2. In this video, what was the most interesting story or idea for you?

- That our brain works better when we take more breaks and let it think in the background.

### Q3. What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

- Active mode is when we focus on the problem itself and diffused mode is when the brain processes it in the background. 

### Q4. According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? Only mention the points.

- Breaking it into small parts.
- Practice regularly
- Remove distractions
- Track progress and improve

### Q5. What are some of the actions you can take going forward to improve your learning process? 

- Reduce phone usage, try to explain concepts in my own words and take breaks when possible.
